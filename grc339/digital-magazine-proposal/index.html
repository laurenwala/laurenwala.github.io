Introduction

Flora

Flora is a sustainable lifestyle magazine. Its target audenience is millenials who are eco-friendly and have a passion towards making sustainable choices for Earth. The magainze contains articles and tips about ways to be more sustainable. It will also include product recommendations and recipes for DIY products.
